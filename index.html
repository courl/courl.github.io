Read before you shoot

Contest rules

Short and simple: keep it fair, keep it safe, have fun.

Photograph your team in front of at least one landmark.

One photo per landmark. Each delegation can hand in one photo per landmark.

Shoot your own originals. Photos must be taken by you during WEOI 2026. No stock images, no AI generations.

Name each file by your delegation and the stop number, e.g. LUX-03.jpg, so we can match it to a delegation and a landmark.

Upload via the Dropbox link before the end of the weekend.

Respect places and people. Follow on-site rules, mind privacy, and never enter restricted areas or climb barriers.

By submitting you allow your photos to be used as promotional material.

The winning delegation is announced after the competition. Everyone in the winning delegation receives their prizes via post.
